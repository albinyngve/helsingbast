Helsingborg – a city of growth

Over the last 25 years, the municipality of Helsingborg has been growing rapidly – from 109 267 citizens in 1990, to 135 344 in 2014.
Since the early ninetees, the population has increased with alomst 16 000: a growth of 14,67% in total. And it keeps growing.
